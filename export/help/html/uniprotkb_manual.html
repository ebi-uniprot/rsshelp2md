Annotations of a UniProtKB entry are structured into the following sections:

  • Function
  • Names & Taxonomy
  • Subcellular location
  • Pathology and Biotech
  • PTM / Processing
  • Expression
  • Interaction
  • Structure
  • Family and Domains
  • Sequence(s)
  • Cross-references
  • Publications
  • Entry information
  • Miscellaneous
  • Similar Proteins
